Apply Tour Campus Contact Us Confirmation Deadline University of Cincinnati's confirmation deadline is May 1, 2025. First-year students who have been admitted to UC’s Uptown campus for the Fall 2025 term will have until May 1, 2025 at 5:00 pm EST to confirm their admission. If you are ready to commit to Cincinnati, confirm your admission by visiting admissions.uc.edu/confirm and paying the confirmation deposit. After confirming admission, you can complete important next steps such as applying for housing and registering for orientation. What are you interested in?
